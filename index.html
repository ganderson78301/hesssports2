Who We Are

More than
a football
group.

Hess Sports Group is a subsidiary of Hess Enterprises, dedicated exclusively to women's professional football. We acquire and grow football clubs — investing not just in winning, but in people.

We build long-term partnerships that develop careers, fund education, and create platforms for life beyond the game. Women's football deserves the same infrastructure and ambition as any elite club in the world.

Built on
Principles.

Everything we do is rooted in a set of core values that guide how we build clubs, support athletes, and grow the game.

i.

Empowerment

We provide athletes with more than a contract — long-term career pathways, education, and platforms that last beyond the final whistle.

ii.

Sustainability

We build for the long-term. Sustainable structures, transparent finances, and responsible growth at every level of our organisation.

iii.

Equality

Women's football deserves elite-level investment and infrastructure. We're committed to closing the gap and setting a new benchmark.

iv.

Innovation

From commercial platforms to athlete development, we take a fresh approach to building successful clubs from the ground up.

v.

Passion

Football is at the heart of everything. We bring genuine passion for the game, the athletes, and the communities our clubs call home.

vi.

Community

Our clubs are rooted in their communities. From Waterford to the international stage, we create belonging and inspire the next generation.
