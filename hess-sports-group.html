Who We Are

MORE THAN
A FOOTBALL
GROUP.

Hess Sports Group is a subsidiary of Hess Enterprises, dedicated exclusively to women's professional football. We acquire and grow football clubs — investing not just in winning, but in people, building long-term partnerships that develop careers, fund education, and create platforms for life beyond the game.

We believe that women's football deserves the same investment, infrastructure, and ambition as any elite club in the world. We're here to prove it.

WATERFORD FC WOMEN

Established in 2024, Waterford FC Women's is a historic first — the first ever female football club in Waterford, Ireland. Playing their inaugural season in 2025, the club is already becoming a symbol of what women's football can be when backed with genuine ambition and investment.

What We Stand For

BUILT ON
PRINCIPLES.

Everything we do at Hess Sports Group is rooted in a set of core values that guide how we build teams, support athletes, and grow the sport.

01

EMPOWERMENT

We provide athletes with more than a contract — long-term career pathways, education, and platforms that last beyond the final whistle.

02

SUSTAINABILITY

We build for the long-term. Sustainable structures, transparent finances, and responsible growth are non-negotiable at every level of our organisation.

03

EQUALITY

Women's sport deserves elite-level investment and infrastructure. We're committed to closing the gap and setting a new benchmark for what's possible.

04

INNOVATION

From commercial platforms to athlete development, we take a fresh approach to building successful enterprises from the ground up.

05

PASSION

Sport is at the heart of everything. We bring genuine passion for the game, the athletes, and the communities our clubs call home.

06

COMMUNITY

Our teams are rooted in their communities. From Waterford to the international stage, we create belonging and inspire the next generation.
